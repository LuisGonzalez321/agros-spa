In the heart of rural Central America lives a courageous family. Just over ve years ago, they made a bold decision to forge a new path, diverging from the cycle of extreme poverty that had de ned their lives for generations. is is the story of Victor Aguilar, his wife Sadia, and their three children, in their journey to enduring prosperity.

Victor spent more than 3 decades working on farms: cutting co ee and planting basic grains.

For years, Victor and his wife struggled as vulnerable, landless farm laborer, trapped in a cycle of poverty.

THE INTERSECTION OF DEEP NEED AND UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY TO BENEFIT MOST FROM AGROS’ HOLISTIC MINISTRY

The opportunity: Victor's life took a dramatic turn when he decided to join the Agros program for landless farmers. Motivated by the dream of owning and working his own land, as well as providing a decent home for his family, Victor found a comprehensive solution to his needs through Agros.

Over the years, the program provided him with access to agricultural land, decent housing, basic services, technical assistance, ongoing productive training, and access to formal markets.

From adversity to success: Victor's participation in Agros has resulted in a remarkable transformation. Over the years, his agricultural production has seen consistent success, leading to a signi cant improvement in his income.

Now, the family of ve lives in their own home, they’ve paid o their land loan, and Sadia looks forward to starting her own business. Victor is an agriculture businessman, a skilled producer who manages the farm, and saves for the future.

Before founding La Bendición village, its 264 acres consisted of a rustic farm.

After replanting with hardy rootstock and resilient new co ee varieties, the area began to thrive.

La Bendición is home for 41 families who have reached $35,000 of average net-worth.

85% of the 41 families own their co ee farms and most of them hire about 8 part-time employees to support the large-scale co ee harvests each season, which generates more than 300 employment opportunities for the region.


Eight years later, the entire community has reached endured prosperity. Families live plentiful lives with hope and optimism for the future and they are an inspiration to others.
